Welcome to La Esquina!

At Bar Corner, Miguel and Rosi warmly welcome you to a charming spot in Huetor Tajar! Since we opened our doors in this picturesque town, we have passionately dedicated ourselves to providing you with experiences that last in your memories.

With Miguel, a true Malaga native, and Rosi, a native of Huetor Tajar, at the helm, you'll find a piece of our essence in every corner of our establishment. We strive to create a warm and vibrant atmosphere where you can enjoy unforgettable moments with your loved ones and lifelong friends.

And we don't forget about the food. From delicious snacks to comforting main dishes, our cuisine blends the best of Malaga's gastronomy with the traditional flavors of Huetor Tajar. Every bite is designed to please your cravings and perfectly complement our exquisite drinks.

But at Bar Corner, we not only care about what we serve on the plate or in the glass, but also about the experience we create around you. Our team, under the direction of Miguel and Rosi, is committed to providing you with friendly and attentive service, making sure you feel at home from the moment you walk through our door.

So come and join us at Bar Corner, where good company, culinary delights, and signature drinks come together to create unforgettable moments in the heart of Huetor Tajar. We are eager to welcome you and share this unique experience with you!
